Who Are We?

Pro-Code Club is a group of highschool students collaborating on projects and learning programming concepts and skills along the way. Learning is the most important aspect of this club, always curious about our what our next problem to solve will be. We all grow when one grows, and we all help those who struggle as well.

What We Do

We build games, apps, and websites. We learn new technologies. Develop a colaborative environment where anyone, no matter what level of programming can participate and grow in their coding experience. Additionally we help with APCSA, APCSP, and Computer Programming
